The Tech Behind Our Error-Free Tools

Building a platform that handles complex document manipulations entirely in the browser is no small feat. It requires leveraging some of the most advanced open-source libraries in the JavaScript ecosystem. We use `pdf-lib` for low-level PDF manipulation, `mammoth.js` for accurate Word document parsing, and `marked` for fast Markdown rendering. By combining these technologies, we've created a seamless experience that rivals traditional desktop software, all within a standard web browser.

This "local-first" approach means that our tools are inherently more stable and secure than their cloud-based counterparts. There are no server-side queues to wait in, and your conversion won't fail just because our server is under heavy load. The work is done by your own computer, ensuring that the speed and reliability of the tool are consistent every time you use it. We've spent countless hours testing edge cases and optimizing performance to make sure that even large, complex files are handled with ease.
