A Privacy-First Approach to Document Management

Most online PDF converters operate the same way: you upload your private documents to an unknown server, wait in a queue, and download the result. This creates significant security vulnerabilities for sensitive information like legal contracts, financial reports, or personal records. We built ConvertPDF to solve this exact problem.

By leveraging modern browser technologies, ConvertPDF processes your files entirely within your own computer's memory. When you drag and drop a file onto our tools, it never travels over the internet. This approach guarantees complete privacy while simultaneously removing artificial restrictions on file size or the number of documents you can convert.

  • Zero Data Tracking: Your files never hit our servers. We cannot see, store, or share your documents because they never leave your device.
  • Bypass Upload Limits: Since nothing is transferred over your network connection, you can process massive PDFs that typical cloud converters would reject.
  • No Hidden Subscriptions: You don't need to create an account, verify an email, or pay a monthly fee. It's an open utility.
  • True Offline Capability: Once you load the tool in your browser, you can disconnect from the internet and continue working securely.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ConvertPDF really private?

Yes. Many online converters upload your files to a server. ConvertPDF uses modern browser technologies to do 100% of the work on your device. No server means no storage and no leaks.

Are there file size limits?

Since nothing is uploaded, we don't have artificial server-side limits. Your only limit is the memory available in your web browser.

Is it free to use?

ConvertPDF is 100% free and open-source. We believe powerful document tools should be accessible to everyone without hidden costs or subscriptions.
