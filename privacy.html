Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

At ConvertPDF, your privacy is our top priority. Our service is designed to be 100% client-side, meaning all document processing happens directly in your web browser. We do not upload your files to our servers. Your documents never leave your device.

Information We Collect

Because ConvertPDF operates entirely in your browser, we do not collect or store your personal documents, files, or sensitive data. We do not require account registration or email addresses to use our tools.

We may collect the following non-personal information through third-party services:

  • Usage analytics: Anonymized page views, session duration, referring sources, and tool usage statistics (via Google Analytics).
  • Advertising data: Google AdSense may use cookies and similar technologies to serve relevant advertisements.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors interact with our site - which tools are popular, how users navigate, and where traffic originates. This helps us improve ConvertPDF for everyone.

Google Analytics collects anonymized data such as:

  • Pages visited and time on site
  • Browser type and device category
  • Geographic region (country/city level, not precise location)
  • Referral sources (search engines, social media, direct)

No personally identifiable information (names, email addresses, file contents) is collected or transmitted to Google Analytics. You can opt out by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Google AdSense and Advertising Cookies

ConvertPDF is supported by advertising through Google AdSense. While your documents are never uploaded (all PDF processing is 100% client-side), displaying ads requires Google to set certain third-party cookies in your browser.

These cookies may include: IDE, NID, DSID, __gads, __gpi, and others set by doubleclick.net, googlesyndication.com, and adtrafficquality.google. They are used by Google and its partners to measure ad performance and serve personalised or contextual advertisements.

Cookie Details

Cookie Provider Purpose Duration
_ga, _ga_* Google Analytics Distinguish unique users, track sessions 2 years
IDE Google (DoubleClick) Ad targeting and frequency capping 1 year
__gads, __gpi Google AdSense Measure ad interactions and prevent repeated ads 13 months
NID Google Store user preferences and personalise ads 6 months

You can opt out at any time:

  • Visit Google Ad Settings to manage personalised advertising preferences.
  • Visit AboutAds.info to opt out of interest-based ads from participating networks.
  • Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

For more information on how Google uses data when you visit sites that use its services, see How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

Your Rights (GDPR / EEA Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). These include the right to:

  • Access - Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification - Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure - Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restriction - Request restriction of processing of your data.
  • Portability - Request transfer of your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection - Object to processing of your personal data for certain purposes.

Since ConvertPDF processes all documents locally in your browser and collects no personal file data, these rights primarily apply to any analytics or advertising data collected by third-party services (Google Analytics, Google AdSense). To exercise these rights with Google, visit Google's Data & Privacy page.

To exercise any rights with us directly, please contact us at convertpdf.contact@gmail.com.

Your Rights (CCPA / California Users)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides you with specific rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Know - You can request details about the categories and specific pieces of personal information collected.
  • Right to Delete - You can request deletion of personal information collected about you.
  • Right to Opt-Out - You can opt out of the "sale" of personal information. ConvertPDF does not sell personal information.
  • Non-Discrimination - We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights.

To submit a CCPA request, email convertpdf.contact@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

ConvertPDF is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us and we will promptly remove it.

Data Retention

Since all document processing happens in your browser, we retain no user files or document data. Analytics data collected via Google Analytics is subject to Google's data retention policies (default: 14 months). Advertising data is retained by Google per their Privacy Policy.

International Data Transfers

Google Analytics and Google AdSense data may be transferred to and processed in the United States or other countries where Google operates. Google participates in data transfer frameworks to ensure adequate protection. For details, see Google's data transfer frameworks.

External Links

Our website may contain links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of such third-party websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of every site you visit.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, your data rights, or our privacy practices, please contact us at convertpdf.contact@gmail.com.
