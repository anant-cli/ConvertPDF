Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

At ConvertPDF, your privacy is our top priority. Our service is designed to be 100% client-side, meaning all document processing happens directly in your web browser. We do not upload your files to our servers.

Information We Collect

Because ConvertPDF operates entirely in your browser, we do not collect or store your personal documents, files, or sensitive data. We do not require account registration or email addresses to use our tools.

Google AdSense and Cookies

We use Google AdSense to serve advertisements on our website. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on your visit to this and other websites on the Internet.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.
