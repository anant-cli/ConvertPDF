Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

At ConvertPDF, your privacy is our top priority. Our service is designed to be 100% client-side, meaning all document processing happens directly in your web browser. We do not upload your files to our servers.

Information We Collect

Because ConvertPDF operates entirely in your browser, we do not collect or store your personal documents, files, or sensitive data. We do not require account registration or email addresses to use our tools.

Google AdSense and Advertising Cookies

ConvertPDF is supported by advertising through Google AdSense. While your documents are never uploaded (all PDF processing is 100% client-side), displaying ads requires Google to set certain third-party cookies in your browser.

These cookies may include: IDE, NID, DSID, __gads, __gpi, and others set by doubleclick.net, googlesyndication.com, and adtrafficquality.google. They are used by Google and its partners to measure ad performance and serve personalised or contextual advertisements.

You can opt out at any time:

  • Visit Google Ad Settings to manage personalised advertising preferences.
  • Visit AboutAds.info to opt out of interest-based ads from participating networks.
  • Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

For more information on how Google uses data when you visit sites that use its services, see How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

External Links

Our website may contain links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of such third-party websites.
