Split PDF pages

Extract pages from a PDF or save selected ranges as separate files. It helps when you only need part of a larger document.

Best for: chapters, invoices, exhibits, form pages, and oversized PDFs.
Input: PDF files.
Output: selected pages or page ranges.

Before you download

Page numbers refer to the order inside the PDF, which may differ from printed labels on scanned documents.

How to split a PDF

  1. Drag your PDF into the tool or click to upload.
  2. Specify the page range (e.g., "1-5" or "10, 12, 15").
  3. Alternatively, select the option to split every page into its own file.
  4. Click Split & Download to get your new PDF segments.

Frequently asked questions

Can I split a password-protected PDF?

You must first unlock the PDF using our Password Protect tool (or provide the password when prompted by the browser) before it can be processed for splitting.

Is there a limit to how many pages I can split?

No artificial limits are imposed by ConvertPDF. However, since the processing happens in your browser's memory, extremely large files (e.g., thousands of pages) may depend on your device's RAM.

Will the links and bookmarks in my PDF still work?

Our splitting engine attempts to preserve internal links and bookmarks that point within the extracted ranges. Links pointing to deleted pages will naturally be removed or become inactive.
