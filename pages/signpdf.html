Sign PDF online

Add a visible signature or approval mark to a PDF in your browser. It is useful for everyday approvals and informal document workflows.

Best for: internal approvals, forms, drafts, and quick sign-offs.
Input: PDF files.
Output: a signed PDF copy.

Before you download

This is not a certificate-based e-signature system. Use your required legal signing platform when compliance rules demand it.

Privacy

The selected file is handled in your browser. It is not uploaded to ConvertPDF servers, and the downloaded result is created on your device.
