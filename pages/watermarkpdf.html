How to watermark a PDF

  1. Select your PDF file or drag it into the tool area.
  2. Enter the text you want to use as a watermark (e.g., "CONFIDENTIAL").

Frequently asked questions

Can I add an image as a watermark?

Currently, our tool supports text-based watermarks. We are working on adding image watermark support (logos, stamps) in a future update. For now, text watermarks offer the best balance of performance and compatibility.

Is the watermark permanent?

Yes. Once you download the watermarked PDF, the text is embedded into the document's content layer. While it can be removed by specialized PDF editing software, it serves as a strong deterrent for unauthorized use.

Can I watermark just the first page?

Our tool currently applies the watermark to every page in the PDF to ensure consistent protection. If you only need it on specific pages, you can split the document first, watermark the parts you need, and then merge them back together.
