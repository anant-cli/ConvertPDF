Add page numbers to PDF

Add page numbers to a PDF so bundles, reports, and submissions are easier to review.

Best for: reports, committee packets, handouts, and document bundles.
Input: PDF files.
Output: a PDF with page numbers.

Before you download

Check the placement before sharing if your original PDF already has footers or page labels.

Privacy

How to add page numbers to a PDF

  1. Upload your PDF file by dragging it into the drop zone.
  2. Select the position (e.g., Bottom Center, Bottom Right, Top Right).
  3. Choose the starting number and the format (e.g., "Page 1" or just "1").
  4. Click Add Numbers & Download to save your updated document.

Frequently asked questions

Can I customize the font and color of the numbers?

Our tool currently uses a standard, highly legible Helvetica font in black to ensure professional results across all document types. Further styling options are planned for future updates.

What if my PDF already has page numbers?

This tool will add a new layer of text over your existing PDF. If your file already has numbers, you may want to adjust the position settings to avoid overlapping.

Is there a limit on the number of pages?

Since the numbering happens in your browser, it can handle hundreds of pages easily. Very large documents (1000+ pages) may take a few extra seconds to process on older devices.
