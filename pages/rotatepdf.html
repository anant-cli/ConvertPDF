Rotate PDF pages

Fix sideways or upside-down PDF pages without uploading the file. Choose the rotation and download a corrected copy.

Best for: scans, forms, labels, and pages from phone cameras or document feeders.
Input: PDF files.
Output: a rotated PDF.

Before you download

Use this before merging files so the final bundle is easier to read.

Privacy

The selected file is handled in your browser. It is not uploaded to ConvertPDF servers, and the downloaded result is created on your device.

How to rotate a PDF

  1. Drag and drop your PDF file or click to select from your device.
  2. Choose the rotation angle (90° Clockwise, 90° Counter-clockwise, or 180°).
  3. The tool will apply the rotation to all pages in the document.
  4. Click Rotate & Download to save your corrected file.

Frequently asked questions

Can I rotate just one page?

Currently, this tool applies the rotation to the entire document. If you need to rotate only specific pages, we recommend using our Split PDF tool first to isolate those pages, rotate them, and then use Merge PDFs to put them back together.

Does rotating a PDF reduce its quality?

No. Our tool modifies the internal page rotation metadata without re-encoding the actual content, ensuring that your text and images remain perfectly sharp and original.

Is this tool free to use?

Yes, ConvertPDF is 100% free and ad-supported. All processing happens on your computer, so we don't have the high server costs that other sites pass on to their users.
