Rotate PDF pages

Fix sideways or upside-down PDF pages without uploading the file. Choose the rotation and download a corrected copy.

Best for: scans, forms, labels, and pages from phone cameras or document feeders.
Input: PDF files.
Output: a rotated PDF.

Before you download

Use this before merging files so the final bundle is easier to read.

Privacy

The selected file is handled in your browser. It is not uploaded to ConvertPDF servers, and the downloaded result is created on your device.

How to rotate a PDF

  1. Drag and drop your PDF file or click to select from your device.
  2. Choose the rotation angle (90° Clockwise, 90° Counter-clockwise, or 180°).
  3. The tool will apply the rotation to all pages in the document.
  4. Click Rotate & Download to save your corrected file.
