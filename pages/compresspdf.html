Reduce PDF file size in your browser before emailing, uploading, or archiving a document. ConvertPDF keeps the file on your device and creates a smaller copy for download.

Best for: large PDFs, scans, reports, forms, and documents that need to fit upload limits.
Input: PDF files.
Output: a smaller PDF copy.

Before you download

Compression results depend on the PDF. Image-heavy scans usually shrink more than text-only documents. If the result is still too large, split the PDF first and compress each part.

How to compress a PDF

  1. Click the drop zone or drag your PDF file into the upload area.
  2. The tool will automatically analyze and optimize the PDF structure.
  3. For image-heavy files, the reduction will be more significant.
  4. Click Compress & Download to save your optimized PDF.

Frequently asked questions

How much can I reduce the PDF size?

This depends on the original file. PDFs created from high-resolution scans or containing many images usually see the most reduction (often 50-80%). Text-heavy PDFs that are already well-optimized may see smaller gains.

Is my PDF uploaded to a server?

No. All compression happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your file never leaves your device, making it safe for confidential documents.

Will I lose quality in my PDF?

Our tool uses lossless compression techniques where possible. For image-heavy PDFs, it may apply subtle downsampling to reach target sizes, but it aims to maintain professional readability.
